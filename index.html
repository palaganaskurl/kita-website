  How to KITA?  
Gusto mo bang kumita?
You’ve come to the right chat.
Ako si KITA, ang iyong finance buddy. Baka iniisip mo, “Napaka-boring naman ‘yun… ‘personal financial goals’ chu chu…” Sige, ipapakita ko sayo paano kumita first and foremost by investing in yourself (aaaawtsss!!!). Sa chika at kuwento, tuturuan kita paano maintindihan ang mundo ng savings at investments. Ikaw mismo ang mag-dedisisyon para sa mga main character natin, parang RPG via chat ganern. Tara, G? PM is key! 😉
Finance made simple
Here at KITA, we believe that Filipinos should have access to financial literacy. That’s why we launched a virtual assistant that’s a category of its own: a quirky Messenger Bot that teaches you how to manage your finances via real-life stories and love lessons. We’re happy as long as you’re learning and helping us spread the word: ngayon, para sa lahat ang KITA!
